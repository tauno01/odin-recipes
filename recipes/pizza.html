Homemade Pizza Recipe

This is a really easy homemade pizza recipe and it tastes like it came from an Italian pizzeria oven!

This is also really cheap and the size of the pizza is really huge!

Ingredients

  • Baking dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Any toppings you want

Steps

  1. First kneel the dough and make it flat like its a pizza
  2. Then spread your tomato sauce evenly on top, and add your cheese and optional toppings
  3. Throw it to oven for 15 minutes on 225 degrees
  4. Enjoy!
