Nistipata

Easy nistipata recipe

Nistipata tastes really good and is cheap

Everybody loves nistipata

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Pasta
  • Ketchup (optional but preferred)

Steps

  1. First boil pasta and start paistaa ground beef
  2. Once pasta boiled, remove water and add paistettu ground beef with pasta and mix
  3. Enjoy good nistipata!
